George Pérez

Comic Book Writer, Artist, Legend!

Illustration of George Pérez with The New Teen Titans - the subject of one of his most memorable comic runs - done by fellow comic book artist José Luis García-López for the DC Comics tribute following Pérez's death.

A Lifetime through Infinite Events

June 9, 1954

Born in South Bronx, New York

Began Drawing with an aspirations of becoming an artist

1959/1960
1973

Became artist Rich Buckner's assistant (first professional work in the comic industry)

Made his professional debut in comics pencilling a 2 page story in Marvel Comics' Astonishing Tales #25

August 1974
December 1975

Along with writer Bill Mantlo, he co-created the first Puerto Rican superhero in Comics - The White Tiger

Had his first significant collaboration with future frequent collaborator, writer Marv Wolfman, on Fantastic Four Annual #14

1979
